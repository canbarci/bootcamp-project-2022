Here are some of my favorite recipes!

Ratatouille

It’s ratatouille season! Ratatouille is a classic end-of-summer French stew that’s fun to say (rat-tuh-TOO-ee) and fun to make. It’s packed with fresh produce: tomatoes, eggplant, zucchini and yellow squash, and bell pepper.

Taco Soup

This Taco Soup recipe is a fall and winter staple! It’s an easy, protein packed soup full of zesty, crave-worthy flavor, plus it’s perfectly hearty and filling. It’s like chili’s soupier cousin and it’s loaded with all the best taco style ingredients!

Beef and Brocolli

Beef and Broccoli is an easy meal that is loaded with fresh broccoli, tender nutrition-packed beef, and the best stir fry sauce.

Banana Bread

This banana bread recipe is so easy to make and super soft and moist! This is the very best way to use up overripe bananas. It makes the bread tender and packed full of flavor.
